Penut and Butter Smoothie

Description

This peanut butter banana smoothie is so refreshing!

The perfect drink to jump start your day in a healthy fashion!

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 2 cups of milk
  • half a cup of peanut butter
  • 2 tbs of honey
  • 2 cups of ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather all the needed ingredients
  2. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, ice, and honey inside blender
  3. Blend until smooth for aprox 30 seconds
  4. Pour and enjoy!
