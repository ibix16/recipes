Teriyaki Salmon Bowl

Description

This Teriyaki Salmon Bowl is the perfect lunch meal!

Ensured to have you feeling energized throughout the day!

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon filets
  • 4 tbs of mustard
  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 3 tbs of olive oil
  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1 small clove of garlic

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Place salmon on pan and brush with mustard
  3. Toss broccoli in bowl and prepare
  4. Cook rice
  5. Cook salmon filets
  6. Prepare bowl and enjoy!
