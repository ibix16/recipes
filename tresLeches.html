Tres Leches Cake

Description

This Tres Leches Cake is the perfect Mexican snack!

Ensured to have you feeling fresh and full!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of yellow cake mix
  • 1 cup of cold water
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 fourth cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Beat together cake mix with water, eggs, and oil
  3. Bake in oven until toothpick comes out clean
  4. Whisk together condensed milk
  5. Prepare and serve!
